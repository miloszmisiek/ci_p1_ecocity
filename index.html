did you know ?

Plastic Pollution

"Every day approximately 8 million pieces of plastic pollution find their way into our oceans. 12 million tonnes of plastic are poured into the ocean every year." (OSPAR, 2009; Eunomia, 2016)

Melting Glaciers

"Scientists project that if emissions continue to rise unchecked, the Arctic could be ice free in the summer as soon as the year 2040 as ocean and air temperatures continue to rise rapidly." (World Wild Life)

Air Pollution

"Air pollution kills 800 people every hour or 13 every minute - more than three times compared to malaria, tuberculosis and AIDS combined each year." (Voice of America)

Chemical Solvents

"Thousands of chemicals from cleaning products are washed into streams and rivers. Some persist in the environment and enter the food chain." (Penn Jersey Paper Co.)
