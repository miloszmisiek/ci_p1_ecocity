did you know...?

Plastic Pollution

Every day approximately 8 million pieces of plastic pollution find their way into our oceans. 12 million tonnes of plastic are poured into the ocean every year.

Melting Glaciers

From 2000 to 2019, the rate of glacier melt accelerated from an estimated .36m per year to .69m per year due to global warming.

Air Pollution

Air pollution kills 800 people every hour or 13 every minute, accounting for more than three times the amount of people who die from malaria, tuberculosis and AIDS combined each year.

Chemical Solvents

Thousands of chemicals from cleaning products are washed into streams and rivers. Some persist in the environment and enter the food chain.
