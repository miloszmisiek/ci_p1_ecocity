how to segrate waste

plastic bags and bottles, food containers, cleaning supplies and cosmetic packagings, bottle tops and lids, milk and juice cartons, soda cans and tin cans, aluminium foil

PLASTICS AND METAL

do not put in:
hazardous waste such as expired medications, batteries, motor oil and its packaging, car parts, paint or varnish cans

paper bags and packages, cartons and paperboard, catalogs, leaflets, old magazines, notebooks, books, stationery (office paper), wrapping paper

PAPER

do not put in:
greasy paper, pads, paper towels, used tissues, coated paper, milk or juice cartons, cement sacks

food and beverage bottles and jars, cosmetics packaging

GLASS

do not put in:
porcelain, pottery, crystals, flower pots, light bulbs, window panes, table glassware, eye-wear or heat-proof glass, electronics, thermometers, prescription bottles, packaging of motor oil or solvent products

food lefovers, fruits and vegetables, green waste (tree and shrub branches), grass, leaves, flowers

BIO-WASTE

do not put in:
animal bones or excrement, ash, soil, stones or dust, particle board, treated wood

animal bones and excrements, egg shells, disposable diapers, contaminated paper, ceramics and textiles, ash (cold), regular filament bulbs

OTHER-WASTE

do not put in:
batteries, bulbs and fluorescent lamps, fluorescent and mercury neon lamps, paints and varnish residues, waste electrical and electronic equipment
